    Welcome to the About Us page of Jesus Breeds Ministries.


    About Us

    Jesus Breeds is a non-profit organization dedicated to spreading the message of love, hope, and faith through various community outreach programs and initiatives. Our mission is to empower individuals and families to lead fulfilling lives rooted in Christian values.


    Our Founder

    Jesus Breeds was founded in 1996 by Bishop Eddy Omoruyi-Okundaye , a visionary leader with a passion for spreading the gospel and transforming lives. With over 20 years of ministry experience, Bishop Omoruyi has dedicated his life to serving God and empowering communities through faith, love, and service.


    Our Vision

    Our vision is to create a world where every individual experiences the transformative power of Jesus Christ. We strive to build a community that reflects God's love and grace, fostering spiritual growth and meaningful connections among all people.


    Our Statement of Faith

    We believe in the authority of Scripture, the power of prayer, and the importance of community. Our faith is rooted in the teachings of Jesus Christ, and we are committed to living out His message of love and redemption.
